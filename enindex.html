About Us

We are a group of passionate and determined students eager for new knowledge and exciting experiences. Our class is a close-knit community where everyone strives for success and supports each other in achieving our goals. What is NIS?

NIS is a network of educational institutions in Kazakhstan, created to provide high-quality education and prepare students for future academic and professional achievements.

NIS are aimed at high academic standards and include in-depth study of subjects such as mathematics, natural sciences, humanities, and foreign languages. The educational process also incorporates innovative methodologies, modern technologies, and international collaboration.

Mission

To develop the intellectual and creative potential and global competencies of an adaptive personality.

Vision

The school provides high-quality education, fostering moral values, leadership, and patriotism in its students.

Values

Respect, Openness, Patriotism, Responsibility, Diligence, Creativity, Honesty, Family Values, Traditions, Health, Well-being, Global Citizenship.
